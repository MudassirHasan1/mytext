ZANONI

by Edward Bulwer Lytton

[1842]
Contents    Start Reading    Text [Zipped]

Zanoni introduced the concept of the wandering, eternal adepts into popular culture, with this tale of tragic love. Bulwer Lytton also wrote the fantasy Vril, The Power of the Coming Race, a prototype for fictions of lost civilizations to come. Zanoni had a huge influence on Theosophists and other 19th century occult groups.
