Self-Suggestion

and the New Huna Theory of Mesmerism and Hypnosis

by Max Freedom Long

[1958]

This is one of Max Freedom Long's latter books. In this we can see the evolution of his ideas, consistent with the views expressed in The Secret Science Behind Miracles. One of the highlights of this book is the review of the self-help scene during the mid-50s, including a mention of Dianetics, as well as General Semantics and other belief systems. Mostly though, this is a practical manual of self-suggestion using the Huna techniques, including detailed instructions as to how the operation works. An appendix discusses the larger context of this in the Huna world.--J.B. Hare
